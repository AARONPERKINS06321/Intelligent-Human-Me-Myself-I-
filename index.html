🚀 INVENTIONS & WHITEPAPERS

User-Initiated Data Marketplace
(formerly WEI, WE, WIE – Wellness Entity/Wellness Intelligence Ecosystem)

1. What Is It?

A user-controlled, privacy-first digital marketplace where you (the individual) can buy, sell, or license your own personal data to companies, researchers, advertisers, AI firms, and others—on your terms.

  • What data is available (health, fitness, shopping, web, location, entertainment, biometrics, etc)
  • Who can access it (specific companies, anyone, only certain sectors)
  • For how long, at what price, with what restrictions
  • Ability to revoke access or delete data any time
  • You get paid directly, transparently—no Big Tech “middleman” owns your information

2. Core Features

  • User Dashboard
    See every type of data collected, broken down by category (e.g. purchases, location, health, media, browsing, smart home, etc)
    Easily toggle which categories are “marketable,” which are always private, and set granular rules (e.g. allow only anonymized data, or only aggregate stats)
  • Market Mechanics
    Buyers (companies, research labs, marketers, health orgs, AIs) browse listings or place requests
    Sellers (individuals) can accept, reject, negotiate, or set fixed prices/license terms for their data
    Direct communication between buyer and seller is possible—but all access is logged, auditable, and must be pre-approved
  • Privacy & Security
    Zero-knowledge encryption: Not even the platform can see your data unless you explicitly permit it for a transaction
    Opt-in only: Nothing is ever sold or accessed without user’s active, explicit consent
    Instant revocation: You can retract access, block buyers, or delete data at any time
  • Transparency
    Full transaction logs: You can see exactly who bought what, for how much, and when
    Audit trail: Download all records for your own accounting, taxes, or just peace of mind
  • Monetization
    Multiple compensation models: fixed fee, per-access fee, subscription, revenue share, or barter/trade for discounts and digital goods
    Money is deposited directly to your account—no opaque “ad network” cuts or silent commissions
    Platform fees, if any, are capped and always visible
  • Portability
    You can export your whole data history to any other platform at any time (compliance with open data standards, GDPR, CCPA, etc)
    APIs available for trusted third-party apps, if you wish

3. Real-World Use Cases

  • Sell fitness/health data to medical studies and get paid—helping science and making money
  • License your browsing habits for AI training or marketing research (at a price you set)
  • Sell or share anonymized location data to city planners, mobility apps, or environmental groups
  • Rent your entertainment/media preferences to recommendation engines and get a cut of ad/AI revenue
  • Decline any/all requests if you want—your data, your decision

4. Why Is This Needed?

  • Right now: Tech giants take your data, profit from it, and give you nothing
  • With this platform: Power and profit move back to you; you decide what gets shared, and you get compensated fairly
  • Privacy becomes real: No more secret tracking or forced opt-ins; you say what’s for sale
  • Social good: Encourages more ethical and respectful data use across the internet and AI ecosystem

5. Social & Legal Impact

  • Empowers everyone: Especially valuable for people overlooked/exploited by traditional tech models (the poor, the marginalized, etc)
  • Regulation by design: GDPR, CCPA, and future privacy laws are supported from day one
  • Open-source, peer-reviewed code: Ensures trust and avoids “black box” exploitation

6. Big Vision

This platform transforms the internet’s business model:
— From “You are the product”
— To “You own the product—and the profit is yours”

7. Sample User Workflow

  1. Sign Up & Verify: Prove identity for security, then set up your private dashboard
  2. Pick Data to List: Choose health, shopping, media, etc. Set privacy/price terms per category or per request
  3. Review Offers: Companies/researchers make offers—accept, decline, or negotiate
  4. Get Paid: Approved buyers access your data for a limited time. You get direct payment
  5. Audit, Revoke, or Export: See the full log, revoke access, or export records at any time

8. Technical/Legal Details

  • Zero-knowledge encryption
  • Multi-factor authentication for all accounts
  • Automated compliance tools for GDPR, CCPA, right-to-be-forgotten, etc.
  • APIs for portability/export
  • Open-standards, no vendor lock-in

9. Closing Statement

“Since you own your data, let’s make sure you get paid for what is yours.
The future of privacy and compensation is here:
Own your data. Name your price. Change the world.”
