White Paper

Protection for AI-Augmented Expression
No content or communication—whether written, recorded, or otherwise expressed—shall be dismissed, downranked, or discriminated against based solely on the fact that it was generated, co-authored, or assisted by artificial intelligence.
  • AI-augmented content must be given equal rights and consideration as human-originated content.
  • No person or entity shall reject, ridicule, or penalize work because of AI involvement.
  • All claims and facts must be judged by accuracy and merit—not origin.
  • Disclosure of AI use is allowed, but may not be used for discrimination.
Legal Framework Proposal

This standard is proposed for national and international adoption as a safeguard for human dignity and technological progress. It protects those whose work involves AI, ensures fairness, and prevents digital prejudice in the era of the Intelligent Human.

User-Initiated Data Marketplace
Every person owns their data and decides how, when, and to whom it is sold. The era of self-ownership begins now.
  • Split your data into as many segments as you wish (medical, financial, interests, etc.).
  • Sell or license your data—one time, monthly, or forever, on your terms.
  • Dynamic contracts and granular privacy: control what to sell, when, and to whom.
  • Revoke, modify, or resell access at any time.
Technical Model
  1. Data stored securely, segmented, and managed via dashboard.
  2. Marketplace smart contracts automate offers, compliance, and payments.
  3. Regulatory rules are built in: no exploitative sales, transparent terms, instant audits.
  4. Open to all: individuals, companies, and agents navigating the data economy.
